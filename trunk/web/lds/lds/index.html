I am what I am

Looking Dwarf is a fictitious company of only one person giving away some stuff. This stuff is not the boldest, best or whateverest of the world, but with some luck -and to our great delight- you could find something useful here.

Looking Dwarf has currently two branches, one for Video Games and other computer-related things, called Looking Dwarf Software -or LDS for short- and the other for publication and marketing of Pen-And-Paper Games called -naturally- Looking Dwarf Games or LDG as you may have guessed.

Friends of the Looking Dwarf

Looking Dwarf is part of a bigger thing, and has several sister companies and organization graviting around it: one of them is the Linguistic and foreign culture Studies Departement (or LSD), which has the project, when it will go back to the ground, to publish a critically-acclaimed (well in the future that is) book about "how to survive with this guide" in many languages.
